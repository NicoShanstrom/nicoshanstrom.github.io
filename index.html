About Me

Hi, I'm Nico, a fitness and health professional turned event coordinator and professional photographer turned software developer

I am a lifelong learner and believe in keeping a 'beginners mind' for there is something to learn from every experience.

My professional career started in 2010 as a sports performance specialist, supporting athletes of all ages and skill levels to become their best self by increasing physical awareness through speed, power, agility, and sport functional training

I wanted to increase my reach with my passion of athletic performance to include all populations as everyone should be able to feel at their highest physical level.

So, I began working with fitness studios and created a private personal training business throughout Miami and Miami Beach.

In 2014 I decided to persue a Masters Degree of Athletic Training, as it seemed like a good tool to round out my fitness and sports performance expertise with evaluation, therapeutics, testing, rehabilitation, and injury prevention.
